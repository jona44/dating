Data Deletion Instructions

We value your privacy and give you full control over your data.

How to Delete Your Data

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, follow these simple steps:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Navigate to the Settings page.
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the page.
  4. Click the "Delete Account" button.

What Happens After Deletion?

When you delete your account:

  • Your profile is immediately removed from the discovery feed.
  • All your matches and conversations are permanently deleted.
  • Your personal information (name, email, age, etc.) is scrubbed from our database.
  • Any uploaded photos are removed from our storage.

Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Questions?

If you have any trouble deleting your data or have questions about our data retention policies, please reach out to us at support@datingapp.com.
